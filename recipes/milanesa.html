Milanesa

Description

This is a Milanesa recipe, an uruguayan and latin-american dish, a classic and a legend amongst adults, children and all kinds of people.

Made with beef and bread crums, it's fame resides in it's quality to be easy to cook and so freaking tasty

Ingredients

  • beef
  • bread crumbs
  • eggs
  • sunflower oil
  • salt

Steps

  1. cut the beef into 1cm thick slices
  2. cut off all the fat from the slices
  3. tenderize the slices with a kitchen hammer
  4. stir the eggs
  5. drench the slices in the eg mixture
  6. heat the oil until it reaches 170ÂºC
  7. cover the slices in bread crumbs and fry them on both sides for some minutes
  8. serve them with fries, mashed potatoes or rice
