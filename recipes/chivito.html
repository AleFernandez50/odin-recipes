Chivito

Description

Traditional uruguayan piece of uruguayan art, the peak of our culture, by the grace of our Gaucho gods.

Made with beef and anything you could imagine, you'll be in the uruguayan Valhalla after eating it

Ingredients

  • beef
  • bread
  • eggs
  • letuce
  • salt
  • tomatoes
  • ham
  • mozzarella
  • olives
  • onion
  • anything

Steps

  1. cook that beef
  2. cover that beef with some hamburger bread
  3. put the letuce
  4. slice and put the tomatoes
  5. slice and put the ham
  6. slice and put the eggs
  7. add the mozzarella
  8. cut and add the onions, the olives and the salt
  9. add anything you want, and EXPERIENCE GLORY
